Privacy Policy for XKCDk

Effective Date: June 30, 2025

Overview

XKCDk ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not require user accounts or registration
  • We do not track user behavior or analytics
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

Information Stored Locally

XKCDk stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Comic Data: XKCD comics you download for offline reading
  • Favorites: Comics you mark as favorites
  • App Settings: Your theme preferences and download settings
  • Cached Images: Temporary comic images for faster loading

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods and is never transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

XKCD.com

XKCDk fetches comic data and images directly from xkcd.com. When you view comics, your device makes requests to:

  • https://xkcd.com/ - To fetch comic metadata
  • XKCD image servers - To display comic images

Please refer to XKCD's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Photo Library Access

XKCDk requests permission to save comics to your photo library only when you:

  • Use the share feature to save comics
  • Explicitly choose to save a comic image

We only access your photo library to save images you choose to save. We do not read or access existing photos.

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to our servers
  • Comic downloads use secure HTTPS connections where available
  • Your data remains under your control at all times

Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can delete downloaded comics at any time through the app
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • No data persists on external servers after app removal

Children's Privacy

XKCDk does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Our app is designed to be safe for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through app updates.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no personal data to:

  • Access, correct, or delete
  • Port to other services
  • Object to processing

All your data remains on your device under your control.
